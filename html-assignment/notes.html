HTML Tags and Their Descriptions

dt><div> ... </div>
A container element used to group other elements for styling and layout purposes.
<h1> - <h6>
Defines headings, where <h1> is the most important and <h6> is the least important.
<p> ... </p>
Defines a paragraph of text.
<cite> ... </cite>
Used to reference the title of a creative work (e.g.,Quoter,Reference or article).
<sub> ... </sub> and <sup> ... </sup>
<sub> is used for subscript text (e.g., H2O), and <sup> is used for superscript text (e.g., 10th).
